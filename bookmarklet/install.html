MedHub Timesheet Filler

Fill 8am–6pm Mon–Fri in one click — works in any browser

Install (one-time setup)

1
Show your bookmarks bar
Chrome: Ctrl+Shift+B / Cmd+Shift+B  |  Firefox: Ctrl+Shift+B  |  Safari: View → Show Favourites Bar
2
Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar:

Drag this button to your bookmarks bar ↓

MedHub Timesheet Filler
Can't drag? Right-click the button → "Bookmark this link" / "Add to Favourites"
3
That's it! The bookmark is now saved in your browser.

How to use (every week)

  1. Log in to singhealth.medhub.com
  2. Go to your timesheet (the week you want to fill)
  3. Click the MedHub Timesheet Filler bookmark in your bookmarks bar
  4. Choose Fill & Submit or Fill Only
Note: If the page redirects (switching to the pull-down view), just click the bookmark once more after the page loads.
